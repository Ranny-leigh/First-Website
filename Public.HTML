The Publics Views On Men's Mental Health

One user was asked "What are your views of Men's Mental Health in the world?" and they responded saying:

"I feel that sometimes, men's mental health can be a bit of a difficult thing to talk about and that some people tend to bottle up their emotions, and don't talk to people when they're stressed, and ruin their own mental health for the sake of being cool. But sometimes men need to hear that being cool isn't worth the hurt you put yourself through, and that they need to talk."
